Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with GradePad?

Simply click "Get Started" and create your account. You can then add your courses and start tracking your grades immediately. No setup required!

Is my data secure?

Yes! Your academic data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties and follow industry-leading security practices.

Can I use GradePad on my phone?

Absolutely! GradePad is fully responsive and works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Access your grades anywhere, anytime.

How do I calculate my GPA?

GradePad automatically calculates your GPA as you add grades. You can also use our built-in GPA calculator to see different scenarios and track your progress.

Can I export my grade data?

Yes! You can export your grade data in PDF or CSV formats for backup, sharing with advisors, or analyzing in external tools.

What grading systems are supported?

GradePad supports letter grades (A-F), percentage scales, and custom grading systems. You can customize the grading scale to match your institution's requirements.

Getting Started Guide

New to GradePad? Follow these simple steps to set up your account and start tracking your grades:

1. Create Your Account

Sign up with your email address and create a secure password. No personal information required beyond what you're comfortable sharing.

2. Add Your Courses

Click "Add Course" and enter your course name, instructor, and any other details you'd like to track.

3. Set Up Assignments

Add your assignments, tests, and projects with their due dates and point values. GradePad will help you stay organized.

4. Track Your Progress

As you receive grades, enter them into GradePad. Watch your progress in real-time with our smart analytics.
